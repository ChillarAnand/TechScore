TechScore Data

TechScore crawls home page of top 1 million alexa websites and stores data in warc format. Raw data can be downloaded from here. We are planning to increase our dataset by crawling upto 20 pages on a website instead of just homepage. This results in improved accuracy of technology detection.

In the long run, we want to crawl all the websites on internet and perform analysis on them. We are also looking into CommonCrawl to reuse the crawl data.
